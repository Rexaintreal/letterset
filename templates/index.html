How it works

01

Print the template

Download our letter sheet and print it. Every character has its own box (Uppercase, lowercase, numbers, punctuation).

02

Write every letter

Fill each box with your handwriting. Use a dark pen for best results. This becomes your font.

03

Upload & Download

Photograph the sheet and upload it. We detect each character, vectorize it and hand you back a .ttf you can install

Tips for a good photo

Good Lighting natural daylight works best. Avoid shadows across the sheet.

Shoot straight down hold the camera directly above the sheet and not at an angle.

Dark pen only ball point or felt tip. Pencil and light ink won't vectorize cleanly.

Flat surface no wrinkles or folds. A crease through a letter box will ruit that glyph.
